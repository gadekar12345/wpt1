Ajay Devgan

About

Born as Vishal Veeru Devgan, he changed his name to Ajay Devgn for the Hindi film industry.
Ajay's father Veeru Devgn is a well-known stunt choreographer and his mother Veena Devgn is a film producer.
Ajay changed the spelling of his surname from Devgan to Devgn on the insistence of his family.
He was born in Delhi, but studied at Silver Beach High School and went to Mumbai's famous Mithibai College.
Has a cousin brother called Anil, who is also a producer. Ajay played a cameo in Anil's film Haal–e–Dil.
The two are often mistaken to be real brothers.
